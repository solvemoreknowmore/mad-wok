Our Story 我们的故事

Founded in 2018, Mad Wok revolutionizes the drive-in experience by bringing authentic Chinese cuisine directly to your car window. We combine traditional cooking techniques with modern convenience.

Our passionate chefs use only the freshest ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Every dish is prepared with precision, care, and a touch of modern creativity that makes our menu truly special.

Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch or enjoying a family dinner, Mad Wok delivers an unforgettable culinary journey through the diverse regions of China - all from the comfort of your driver's seat.
